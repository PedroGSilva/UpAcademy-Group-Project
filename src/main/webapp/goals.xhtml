Our Goals!

From Scrap to Paper...

E.MED is a platform created to benefit and optimize the time health and administrative professionals take to sort and allocate patients to the correct emergency sections. It is designed to not only improve the user experience, but also automatize the patient triage process as well.

Based on the Manchester Triage System, we hope our platform ensures an intuitive and fast paced interface to you while providing you the best experience possible

E.MED started off as a project developed during UpAcademy 2017 and we hope that in one day it grows further.

Thank you for sharing this experience with us.
